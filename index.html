Do's and Don'ts of Web Accessibility

Images should always have specific alt tags:

Colors that lack in contrast or cause eye strain should be avoided. Colorblind users should also be considered during the design process.

  • Yellow
  • Light Grey
  • Cyan

Fonts should be easily read by users with various levels of visual abilities. Decorative fonts should only be used as headers --if at all.

  • Mrs. Eaves XL Serif
  • Proxima Nova
  • Ironwood
  • Neplus
